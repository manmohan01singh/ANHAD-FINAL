Sacred Scripture

Sri Sarbloh Granth Sahib Ji is also known as Manglacharan Puran or Sri Manglacharan Ji. Primarily revered by Nihang Singhs who attribute its authorship to Sri Guru Gobind Singh Ji.

📜

ਸਰਬਲੋਹ ਗ੍ਰੰਥ ਬਾਰੇ

Sri Sarbloh Granth is a sacred text attributed to Guru Gobind Singh Ji, revered especially by the Nihang Singhs. It emphasizes the worship of Akal Purakh as the All-Steel Lord of the Universe.

📖
