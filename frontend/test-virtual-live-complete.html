What is "Virtual Live"?

Virtual Live means the audio behaves like a real live radio stream. When you pause for 10 minutes and resume, it should jump forward 10 minutes to stay synchronized with the "live" timeline.

✅ CORRECT: Pause at 4:20, wait 10 min, resume at 4:30
❌ WRONG: Pause at 4:20, wait 10 min, resume at 4:20 (cached)
